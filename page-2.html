Koenigsegg One:1

My all time favorite car is the Koenigsegg One:1. There is something about the exclusivity of it, and the lines and raw power that makes it my favorite. It was the first production vehicle to have a one to one power to weight ratio, hence the name. There were only 7 examples produced, and one was a company only production. There is only one in North America and I was lucky enough to be able to see it in person. There is a dealership five minutes from my house in Illinois that had it when I went and took pictures. That dealership is one of three in the entire United States. Seeing my dream car in person was a truly exciting time.
